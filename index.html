The problem

You have the data. You're missing the picture.

For most women navigating perimenopause and menopause, the care system is fragmented by design. A GP has eight minutes. A private menopause clinic charges £200–£465 per visit. Each practitioner sees a slice. The interdependencies — sleep, cortisol, HRT, metabolism, microbiome — live in the spaces between appointments.

Emilie was built because one of her founders needed it. She had the blood work, the DUTCH test, the Mira readings, the wearable data, and a thoughtful team of practitioners. Each piece worked. The combination didn't exist.

That's the product.

The approach

Three things, done properly.

The thoroughness of a private specialist, the patience of an AI that knows your history, and the human judgement of a credentialed practitioner who has done this work for a decade.

Multi-source data

Your blood work, DUTCH metabolites, Mira readings, DNA panels, and wearable streams — woven together over time into one complete view of your health, in your hands. Not another dashboard. A foundation to build care on.

AI that knows you

An AI consultant grounded in your own physiology and a curated functional-medicine knowledge base. Reasons from your data, not averages. Remembers context. Cites every claim.

Real human coaches

When you need a person, a vetted IMH-C or functional-medicine practitioner is one tap away. Six-session programmes are the unit of real clinical progress. Always a human in the loop.

What makes us different

The integrated position is unfilled.

If you've been navigating perimenopause, you've probably collected pieces of a solution — a lab subscription, a tracking app, a telehealth visit, a coach. The pieces are useful. The picture isn't. Emilie is the picture.
